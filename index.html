About Me

Born in Sibenik, Croatia in 1986. I have spent years as an Army Infantry Officer before moving into a career of Web Development. While serving in ISAF mission in Afghanistan (Sept '12 - Mar '13) I came into contact with surveilance technologies such as Persistent Threat Detection System (Aerostat). Having an extensive collaboration with the managers of these technologies made me realize that one day I could make a successful transition into the IT industry.

Recently, I ventured in an area of fiction writing. "Hell is War: Warriors in Hell" is the title of my first novel. Halfway futurist yet I'm a complete boxing enthusiast. While working on my second novel I split time between Mississauga, Ontario and Skradin, Croatia.
